Product Design 101: Exploring the Design Process with Figma

The role of product designers in modern companies has a high impact in various fields, from consumer tech (eg. social media, navigation, shopping) to specialized industry applications (eg. software for health practitioners and engineers.) This hands-on workshop will cover an introduction to the most popular tool used to prototype product solutions - Figma! You will learn about basic tools and features, before diving into a practical application where you can exercise your creative problem solving to learn more about the design process, as well as a few advanced features of Figma.

Physical Computing with Raspberry Pi Pico and Neopixel Light Strips

In this exciting workshop, beginners to programming will be introduced to the popular Raspberry Pi Pico microcontroller and will be progressively taught how to program the Pico using the CircuitPython language. You will program the Pico to control colourful Neopixel light strips and will also interact with your programs using digital switches (buttons) and analog potentiometers (knobs). Building on everything that has been taught, the workshop will conclude with a fun game.
