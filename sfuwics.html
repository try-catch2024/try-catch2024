WiCS at SFU

Established in 2002, Women in Computing Science (WiCS) strives to promote and support women in Computing Science, build a strong network of friendly faces for women, and challenge the biases faced by women in Computing Science. WiCS has more than 250 active members. We run programs and hold events all 3 semesters of the year, including a faculty mentor lunch, Fall mentorship program, company tours, technical interview workshops, and many socials. For more information, please visit the WiCS website. You can reach WICS by email: wics@sfu.ca
